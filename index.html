Get Ready for the most entertaining night of your life!
"What's this all about?"

If you like dogs and starchy snacks, then science guarantees that you are going to love our organization. We host monthly meetings where members are given an opportunity to connect with rescued canines while enjoying a delicious piece of polish quisine. Our hope is to find a lovely home for as many of these wayward animals as possible. However, interest in adoption is not a requirement for attendance. Your time and affection alone are priceless for these pups.

Want to join us?
"Let's get some info.""

We'd love to see you sometime soon, but first we have to know a few things. Our signup process is quick and easy taking roughly 1.5 minutes to complete. The information gathered is used solely to enhance your experience and kept 100% private. Once you're done, you will be provided with a meeting time and location that best suits your availability. Click here or click the signup tab above to get started now!
